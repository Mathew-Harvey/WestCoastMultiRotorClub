About Our Club

Founded by passionate FPV pilots

WCMRC is Perth's ultimate FPV Drone Racing Club! If you love the thrill of high-speed drone racing or just want to try not crashing into a tree on your first lap, we've got you covered! We hold epic fortnightly race events throughout the year, plus casual beginner-friendly race and freestyle days—because everyone has to start somewhere (even if that means a few crash landings).

Our race setup? Next-level. We're talking top-tier gates, insane obstacles, and precision race timing gear—all kept in peak condition by the legends of the WCMRC Committee. Our home base at Thomas Kelly Pavilion, Kwinana, is fully equipped with a shaded flying area, power to keep those LIPO's topped up, toilets, a canteen (for mid-race snacks), a licensed sports bar (because even drones need pit stops), TV screens and ground stations to keep up with the action.

Our mission is to promote the exciting sport of FPV drone racing, foster innovation in drone technology, and build a supportive community of pilots who share a passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the air.
