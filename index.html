About Our Club

Founded by passionate FPV pilots

West Coast Multi Rotor Club was established in 2016 by a small group of drone enthusiasts who sought to create a community where pilots of all skill levels could meet, learn, and race together. What started as casual meetups has evolved into one of the region's premier drone racing clubs.

Our club is affiliated with the National Drone Racing Association and hosts regular races that comply with official competition standards. We pride ourselves on maintaining a welcoming atmosphere for beginners while providing challenging courses for experienced pilots.

Our mission is to promote the exciting sport of FPV drone racing, foster innovation in drone technology, and build a supportive community of pilots who share a passion for pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the air.
