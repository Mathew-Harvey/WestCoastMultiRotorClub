About Our Club

Premier Drone Racing Experience

Founded in 2018, Velocity Drones Racing Club has quickly established itself as the leading drone racing community in the region. Our mission is to promote the sport of drone racing, foster technical innovation, and build a vibrant community of pilots, engineers, and enthusiasts.

We offer state-of-the-art racing facilities, expert coaching, regular competitions, and a supportive community for racers of all skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, there's a place for you at Velocity Drones.

Our club is recognized by the International Drone Racing Association and regularly hosts qualifying events for national and international competitions.
