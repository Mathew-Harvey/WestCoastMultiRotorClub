Why Capacitors Are Essential in FPV Drone Builds

In the world of FPV drones, a $2 capacitor can mean the difference between a flawless flight and an expensive trip to the repair bench. Learn how voltage spikes, electrical noise, and current surges can destroy your electronics, and why a simple capacitor is your quad's best defense.

What You'll Learn:

  • Voltage Spike Protection: How capacitors protect ESCs and flight controllers from motor-induced voltage spikes
  • Video Feed Filtering: Eliminate electrical noise for cleaner FPV video transmission
  • Low-ESR Importance: Why Equivalent Series Resistance matters for effective filtering
  • Proper Placement: Where to mount capacitors for maximum protection
  • Component Selection: Choosing the right capacitance and voltage ratings for your setup
